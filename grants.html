JAMUN Grants

Supporting Access and Participation

JAMUN offers grants to help schools and students participate in Model United Nations who might otherwise face financial or logistical barriers.

Our Grants

  • JAMUN Access Grant: Provides funding to schools starting their first Model UN team, supporting them all the way from creation to competition.
  • Student Participation Grant: Helps individual students with the costs of registration, travel, and participation to attend JAMUN conferences.

How to Apply

To apply for a JAMUN grant, please fill out the application form linked below. Our team reviews applications on a rolling basis and will contact you within two weeks.
