What We Do

The Premier Middle School Model UN Conference

The Junior Assembly of the Model United Nations is a nonprofit organization dedicated to cultivating the next generation of global leaders and diplomats. Our Model United Nations conferences provide an unparalleled opportunity for middle school students to delve into complex international issues, hone their public speaking and negotiation skills, and develop a profound understanding of global affairs through our twice-yearly Model UN conferences. Join us in shaping informed, articulate, and responsible citizens ready to tackle the world's most pressing challenges.

Middle Schoolers Lead Today

JAMUN gives middle schoolers the space to become leaders, creating a platform for them to speak confidently, think critically, and work collaboratively. As they take on the roles of delegates, students learn to negotiate, solve problems, and lead discussions on real-world issues. Through this experience, they build the courage, communication skills, and sense of responsibility that define strong, thoughtful leaders.

So They Can Shape Tomorrow

The skills students build at JAMUN prepare them to tackle the complex challenges of tomorrow. Whether addressing global conflicts, climate change, or social injustice, these future leaders will be equipped to listen, lead, and find creative solutions that make a real difference in the world. At JAMUN, we don't just build better delegates; we build better students, thinkers, and problem solvers.
