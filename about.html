What is JAMUN?

The Junior Model United Nations (JAMUN) is an immersive academic simulation that empowers middle and high school students to explore global issues through the lens of diplomacy and international cooperation. Over the course of the conference, delegates sharpen their public speaking, negotiation, and critical thinking skills while engaging in thoughtful debate on real-world challenges. Guided by a team of passionate student leaders and educators, JAMUN inspires young participants to become informed, confident, and empathetic global citizens.

Our Mission

The mission of the Junior Model United Nations is to cultivate the next generation of global leaders by providing an unparalleled platform for diplomatic education. We are dedicated to fostering critical thinking, empathetic understanding, and the collaborative skills necessary to address the world's most pressing challenges.

Educate

We believe in learning by doing. Our committees are designed to challenge delegates to delve deep into complex global issues, moving beyond headlines to understand the intricate nuances of international relations and policy-making.

Empower

JAMUN is a training ground for essential life skills. We provide a supportive environment where students find their voice, build confidence in public speaking and negotiation, and master the art of building consensus among differing viewpoints.

Inspire

We aim to ignite a lifelong passion for global engagement. By connecting students with diverse perspectives and complex problems, we inspire them to become informed, active, and compassionate citizens of an interconnected world.
