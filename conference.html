Conference

November 1-2, 2025

Thank you for your interest in attending JAMUN III. Registration opens on May 1st, 2025 and will remain open until September 24. Registration operates on a first-come, first-served basis. Our administrative team will confirm your delegation's position assignments and number of allotted spots by early-October.

To begin, click the Register button in the top-right of the page. Registration confirmation and further instructions will follow. Please reach out to our registration team at creayporter@gmail.com with any questions.

Participation in JAMUN conferences has a small fee which helps us cover the essential costs of running the conference, such as materials, venue space, and other logistical needs. We work hard to keep the fee low while still providing a high-quality experience for all participants.

Fees, Refunds, and Financial Aid

Delegate Fee: $15 per student

Faculty Advisor Fee: No fee! $0 per advisor

All fees must be paid in full by November 1, 2025. Payment instructions will be included in your confirmation packet.

Refunds are available until October 7, 2025. After this deadline, we cannot process cancellations for refunds.

We are proud to offer financial aid to ensure that all students have the opportunity to participate. If your delegation requires support, please email alexfumerton@gmail.com with your request by November 1, 2025.
