Donate to JAMUN

Your Support Makes a Difference

JAMUN relies on generous donations to provide a meaningful Model United Nations experience to youth across the country. Your contribution helps cover venue costs, materials, grants, and more.

Why Donate?

Although JAMUN is a one-of-a-kind learning and growing experience for the hundreds of kids who compete in it every year, there are thousands of students who can not come to JAMUN because their school has a hard time finding the time and resources to put together a team. For just $600, we are able to work directly with these schools to start their first JAMUN team with the help of the JAMUN Access Grant.

How Your Donation Helps

  • Support the JAMUN Access Grant to help schools start their first Model UN team
  • Cover venue costs and materials for conferences
  • Provide resources and training for students and coaches
  • Enhance the overall JAMUN experience for all participants

JAMUN is an Illinois nonprofit organization and has filed Form 1023 for recognition as a 501(c)(3) public charity with the IRS. If approved, our tax-exempt status will be retroactive to our date of formation, pursuant to IRS Publication 557. Donations made while our application is pending may be tax-deductible. Please consult your tax advisor.
