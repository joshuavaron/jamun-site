Directive Outline

Title (Be creative!)
Ex: Developing Unforgettable Simulations for Thoughtful International Negotiations (DUSTIN)
Committee Name
Ex: JAMUN Secretariat
Sponsors (Main authors)
Ex: Dustin, Joshua, Emma
Signatories (Delegates that want the directive to be brought to the floor to debate)
Ex: Sahana, Sam, Miranda, Abby

Operative Clauses

Beginning
A verb in the simple present (Direct, Move, Create, etc.)
Content
The sponsors’ proposed solutions such as:
Obtaining funding
Building an army
Creating new institutions
Etc.
Ending
End each clause with a period.
Ex: Create a national university to educate Canadian citizens in Law and Economics.
